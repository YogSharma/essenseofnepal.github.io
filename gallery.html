Sagarmatha, also known as Mount Everest, is the highest peak in the world, standing tall at 8,848 meters (29,029 feet). Located in the Himalayas on the border between Nepal and Tibet (China), Sagarmatha is a breathtaking marvel of nature, attracting adventurers and mountaineers from around the globe to conquer its majestic summit. The mountain's sheer grandeur and awe-inspiring beauty make it an iconic symbol of human endeavor and the wonders of the natural world.

In the traditional way of filling water in a "gagri," water is poured gently into its narrow neck from a pot or bucket. The process requires careful handling to avoid spillage and maintain balance while carrying the filled gagri on the head or shoulders. This age-old practice remains a significant part of daily life in many South Asian communities.

Stupas are ancient and sacred structures found in various Buddhist regions, serving as places of worship and meditation. Typically, a stupa consists of a hemispherical dome with a square base, symbolizing the enlightened mind of the Buddha. People often walk around the stupa in a clockwise direction, performing "kora" or circumambulation, as an act of reverence and spiritual practice. These majestic structures hold great cultural and religious significance, attracting pilgrims and visitors seeking peace and spiritual solace.

As one of the most pristine and remote lakes in the country, it offers a serene escape for trekkers seeking untouched natural beauty. With its cultural significance and breathtaking landscape, Phoksundo Lake is a hidden gem for adventurous souls.

Lumbini, located in the Rupandehi District of Nepal, is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites for Buddhists around the world. It is the birthplace of Siddhartha Gautama, who later became known as Buddha, the founder of Buddhism. The holy site of Lumbini is adorned with monasteries, stupas, and the Mayadevi Temple, attracting pilgrims and visitors seeking spiritual enlightenment and historical significance.

Pashupati is an ancient Hindu temple complex in Kathmandu, Nepal, dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is a revered pilgrimage site, attracting devotees from all over the world to participate in sacred rituals and ceremonies. As a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Pashupati holds immense cultural and religious significance, embodying the essence of Nepal's rich spiritual heritage.
